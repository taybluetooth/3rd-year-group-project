FLOCK

Flock is a photo-sharing social media platform providing a safe outlet for users to share photos of animals, objects, landscapes and much more. The app provides the ability to share comments, earn points from other users, and allow location sharing for other users to visit the same locations that photos were taken.

We developed this platform with the aim of building a fun and safe community revolved around photography, where all photography skills are rewarded, from beginners to professionals. To help curate this safe environment, moderators are employed to ensure all content shared is within our guidelines and no personal information is shared, as well as ensuring all users are treated respectfully and fairly.

The platform also incorporates several game-like features to encourage users to remain active and engaged. These include a points system based off "likes" received by other users, as well as an achievement system and frequent user challenges.
